OpenProject Analytics

This project analyzes milestone structure, effort estimation, and risk exposure based on OpenProject exports. The data pipeline is implemented in Python, while the visualization layer uses D3.js.

Project Context

The goal is to transform project management data into actionable insights. Milestones, story points, and risks are combined into quantitative metrics to support prioritization and decision-making.

What: Automated risk & effort analysis from project exports. Why: Enables data-driven prioritization and early identification of high-impact milestones.

Milestone Overview

Scoring Model

Combines effort (story points) and risk to identify which milestones deserve priority attention.

The combined score is computed as:

$$\text{Combined Score} = \text{Story Points} \times \text{Risk Exposure} $$

where the risk exposure is defined as the sum of weighted risk probabilities:

$$ \text{Risk Exposure} = \sum_i w_i $$

Each risk is assigned a weight based on its probability level (e.g. low, intermediate, high).

Methodology

  • Parsing and normalization in Python
